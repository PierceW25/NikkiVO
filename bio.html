Nikki Gusti, Voice Actor

The Many Flavors of Nikki

(for the Backstory Junkies)

Nikki Gusti discovered her love of the arts at a young age with her creative and energetic personality. Her inquisitive nature and love of literature aided her quest for knowledge, exposing her to the wondrous world outside Richmond, Virginia. Always eager to travel, Nikki imagined a career that could take her around the world. Theater in high school provided fun playtime for imaginative use of her various skills, comedic wit, sarcasm, and voice impersonations! Her explorative insight, adaptability, and authenticity allow her to approach any project optimistically and utilize her extensive skill-set to create the voiceover experience her clients envision.

Born and raised in Virginia, Nikki’s love of hospitality and food grew out of her family gatherings surrounded by boisterous juggernauts, joyful laughter, and delicious family feasts! While achieving “Best Actress in a One-Act” her senior year in David Ives’ Mere Mortals demonstrated her thespian talents, Nikki chose the culinary path. Higher education took her first to New York and then to Las Vegas, with a study abroad adventure in Italy; cementing her love of travel and cultural exploration.

"A fresh female voice actor with a diverse skill set and global appeal"

"VO Mid-Atlantic, North American Accent"

"Authentic Conversational Speaking Style"

Nikki’s charismatic personality and loquacious demeanor earned her a UNLV Teaching Assistant position. Her teaching experience was enriched during her time as a corporate trainer with MGM Resorts International. Her trips to Holland, the UAE, and Turkey also helped with adapting to many situations, utilizing her inviting charm and hospitality to effectively communicate and enrich her cultural awareness.

Nikki enjoys variety, accepts a challenge, and is a creative problem solver with adaptable improv skills! When she’s not working, you can find her cooking, consuming true crime podcasts & documentaries, crafting, reading, bellydancing, gardening, golfing, or giggling with her Girl Gang!
